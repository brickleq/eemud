Endorian Empires

Welcome to the Semi-Official Endorian Empires MUD Home Page. Endorian Empires is a modified Circle 3.0 Multiple User Dimension based upon the works of J.R.R. Tolkien. It provides a wonderful atmosphere for players to get together and talk, fight, eat, drink, and adventure, and it's all available over the Internet, at absolutely no cost whatsoever. Currently, Endorian Empires is in the Alpha stage of construction, which means that it is still in the very early stages of being built. Right now there aren't many zones to explore, and there are still some major coding projects to be completed. But not to worry! There is an expert team of builders and coders working 'round the clock to get the MUD ready to be played. This does not mean that you can't log on. Not at all. You are, in fact, encouraged to. Telnet to handel.seattleu.edu 7777, and follow the instructions you are given. Feel free to ask for help: there is a large staff of Immortals just waiting to help out new users. So enter Endorian Empires... A whole world awaits you.
