Frequently Asked Questions

If your question is not answered here, please feel free to Email Us.

When I click 'here' to connect, it gives me an error message.  Why is this?

This means that your web browser does not have a telnet application reqistered with it.  To download a telnet application, go look around at Stroud's CWSAPPS List.  Zugg's MUD Client, a telnet application made specialy for MUD playing, is also available.

I can get connected to the MUD, but when I don't know what name to enter.  Should I use guest or new?

No.  Just enter the name that you wish to use.  If it asks you for a password, that means that the name you want is already taken.  Re-login and try another one.  Most of the names from Lord of the Rings are already taken.

Once I get connected to the MUD, are there any rules I need to be aware of?

YES!  Once you are in the game, type 'policy' and hit enter to view the rules.  Ignorance is no excuse for breaking them, and it is your responsibility to be familiar with them.

I've heard that there is a 'snoop' command that lets immortals see what I type.  Is this true?

Yes, it is.  However, only the highest and most trusted immortals have access to it.  

Isn't this a violation of my right to privacy?

No.  When you enter the MUD, you are on our property.  We have a right to monitor any activity on our property.  The 'snoop' command allows us to catch many policy breakers and multiplayers that we would otherwise not be able to catch, thereby giving you a better, fairer, cleaner atmosphere to play in.

Do immortals enjoy killing and freezing players?  I've hear that they do things like that alot...

No, we don't enjoy punishing players.  Often that is the only way to get a policy breaker to stop bugging people.

I've read the policy, and it seems that lots of players have no regard for it.  Does this mean that it isn't enforced?

No, one of the immortals can only be in one place at one time.  If you see the policy being broken, please report the offender.

The MUD appears as if it is still incomplete.  Many of the zones are not Tolkien-oriented, and some of the spells and skills are not in full operation.  Why is this?

The MUD is still in the Alpha stage of construction.  There are many thing still to be done.  We're working on that as fast as we can.
